🚯 Plastic-Free Campaign

Raising awareness on reducing plastic and promoting sustainable alternatives

🌍 Why Go Plastic-Free?

  • Plastic takes over 500 years to decompose
  • 8 million tons of plastic enter our oceans every year
  • Harms marine life and contaminates food chains
  • Leads to pollution and health risks in humans
  • Contributes to climate change through toxic emissions

🌿 Sustainable Alternatives

  • Use cloth/jute bags instead of plastic bags
  • Switch to bamboo or metal straws
  • Carry reusable water bottles and containers
  • Opt for paper, bamboo, or steel utensils
  • Shop in bulk to reduce packaging waste

📢 How We Spread Awareness

We organize clean-up drives, school workshops, social media challenges, and plastic-free day events to educate and inspire people to switch to sustainable living.

🤝 Join the Campaign

  • Become a volunteer for our events
  • Organize plastic-free workshops in your school or office
  • Take the "Say No to Plastic" pledge
  • Promote eco-friendly habits in your community

💡 Remember

Every small step counts. By refusing plastic today, we protect our planet tomorrow. Choose green. Live clean.
