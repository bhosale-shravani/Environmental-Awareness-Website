🌱 Green Education Initiative

Workshops in schools and colleges on eco-awareness and climate action

🎓 What is Green Education?

Green Education promotes environmental awareness among students through engaging workshops, seminars, and projects. Our goal is to inspire the next generation to act responsibly and sustainably.

📚 Topics We Cover in Workshops

  • Introduction to Climate Change and Global Warming
  • Plastic Pollution and Ocean Clean-up
  • Deforestation and Tree Plantation Importance
  • Water Conservation and Rainwater Harvesting
  • Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy
  • Waste Management and Composting
  • Sustainable Lifestyle Practices
  • Green Careers and Innovation in Sustainability

🧑‍🏫 Types of Sessions Conducted

  • Interactive Workshops – Presentations with videos and quizzes
  • Hands-on Activities – Tree planting, DIY recycling crafts, compost demos
  • Guest Lectures – Talks by environmentalists, scientists, or NGOs
  • Student Competitions – Poster-making, debates, eco-quiz, slogan writing
  • Eco Walks & Clean-up Drives – Engage students with action-based learning
