🌳 Tree Plantation Drive

Organizing local drives to increase green cover in cities and villages

🏡 What is Tree Plantation?

Tree plantation is the process of planting trees for ecological and environmental restoration. By organizing tree plantation drives, we aim to restore nature, improve biodiversity, and fight pollution — one tree at a time.

📌 Why Tree Plantation is Important

  • Reduces Air Pollution
  • Improves Rainfall
  • Prevents Soil Erosion
  • Supports Wildlife
  • Reduces Global Warming

🤝 How We Organize Tree Plantation Drives

We plan and execute local plantation campaigns in:

  • Urban areas — schools, parks, streets, offices
  • Rural areas — barren lands, farms, village roads
  • Institutions — colleges, government offices, hospitals

Key Steps:

  • Site Selection
  • Permission & Partnership
  • Tree Selection
  • Community Engagement
  • Planting & Monitoring
